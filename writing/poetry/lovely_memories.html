Lovely Memories

Sitting on the porch this morning with a cup of tea I saw passing by a now old, cozy memory Of you and myself talking long through the night Not a single issue between us to catalyze a fight. Your voice and literature so kind and warm Could easily protect me through disastrous storms And your eyes – so loving that the slightest Would make a home out of a house missing a kitchen sink And it’s not just one moment i remember and treasure of us together But all my heartstrings you’ve plucked and with yours you’ve tethered That even if i were to be burnt to ash and scattered You and my faith would be all that ever mattered. In the evening when i baked the pie you adored I saw the scent path you followed with your nose past the bedroom door … All these beautifully bittersweet memories renewed and relived I will wait as long as it takes for our happily ever after again to begin.
