Daisies II

What more could i want Than sleep in a garden Where daisies give hugs to everyone who walks in Dreaming of peace and Seeing beauty in everything Intelligent design is nothing less than amazing. Eternal smile from passing a temporary test Ending heartbreak, desiring rest The world is blind to the next life Unimaginable relief and ease What they feel is all they can believe Cuz all they focus on is their own strife But ignore the terror they soon will face Melted skin, scorching water to drink Severe torture cuz right now they’d rather not think Of the eternal future, they’re absorbed in the illusive instant.
