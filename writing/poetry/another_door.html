Another Door

From one place to another I move with the wind of my emotions the consequences of my actions I close the doors I choose to not walk through but I sometimes wish I didn't have to walk through another. Let me sit in this room for a little bit longer let me enjoy these people for who they are let us grow together, improve each other let us rejoice in the blissful melodies of each other's voices i want to fall into your hugs, sink into your affection forever. I make a mistake and a new door appears hesitating as I peer back at my peers. I'll miss all the things we discussed, played, and laughed at together. In this world, beautiful things don't last forever. Those brief moments are what I'll think about as I draw my last breath Falling through the timelines before my death. Thank you for giving a shallow heart some depth.
