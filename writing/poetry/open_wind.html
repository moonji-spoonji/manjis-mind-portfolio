Open Wind

Flurries of leaves within beelines before me, dancing along to the autumn tune I want to connect but my heart is heavy paralyzed body, unsure of what next to do. Sweet, gentle hands of the pansy gale blowing stray hairs aside. The sun smiles joyously until it retires to let the moon shine. An intoxicating light beam lifts me to the trees, tells me to let go and sail te open breeze.
