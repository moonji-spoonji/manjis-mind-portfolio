Wake Up

A dream is a dream and nothing more. Open your eyes my dear, close that door. Reality begs for your presence here A life is not lived in the mind, i fear Happiness is more than a moment or a while Just as joy is not shown only by a smile. Sure it’s nice to think of worlds with cotton candy trees But fantasies create fanatics of things that will never be. Disappointment then leads to self-induced depression Since expectations were raised by your artistic expression Pessimism attached to all your world views This is the delusion resulting from the path you might choose. Open your eyes and live in the world around you If you grope for what lies in the dark, you’ll have nothing to hold on to.
